Our Vision

Every company signs contracts they don't fully understand — buried liabilities, auto-renewal traps, one-sided indemnity clauses, compliance gaps, and hidden obligations. ZeroTrace transforms every contract into structured, queryable intelligence— flagging risk, tracking obligations, and answering legal questions instantly, ensuring no deadline, clause, or exposure ever slips through unnoticed.

From identifying risks to tracking obligations and answering questions in seconds, ZeroTrace enables faster, safer, and more informed decisions.
