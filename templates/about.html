About ZeroTrace

Our Vision

Every company signs contracts they don't fully understand — buried liabilities, auto-renewal traps, one-sided indemnity clauses, and compliance gaps. Legal teams are stretched thin, and SMEs often have no legal team at all. ZeroTrace turns every contract into structured, queryable intelligence — flagging risk, tracking obligations, and answering questions instantly — so no clause, deadline, or exposure ever slips through unnoticed. We are not a chatbot bolted onto PDFs; we are a system of record for legal risk.

Our Mission

To give SMEs and legal teams an AI-native platform that reads, understands, and monitors every contract they sign — surfacing risk, tracking obligations, and answering questions in seconds instead of hours.
